Our Programmes

The foundation addresses the need of the underprivileged children via a three-pronged approach by forming the below three programs to address the basic necessites of a child.


Objective

Manages Budimas Orion, a shelter to accommodate underprivileged children located in Senawang, Negeri Sembilan. (BHCF) Extending monetary support to 60 charitable homes nationwide with over 2,500 orphaned and underprivileged children. (BHCF) Providing breakfast for over than 4,000 children from poor families in 113 public schools nationwide. (BFCF) Support refugee children by providing them with the breakfast every school-morning. (BFCF) Building and managing children’s libraries in rural villages as well as in Orang Asli settlements. (BECF)


Mission

To provide guidance and funding in support of the welfare and the well-being of orphans and underprivileged children in Malaysia.


Our Vision

Giving children a future of love and hope.
