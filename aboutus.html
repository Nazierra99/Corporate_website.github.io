About Us

The Budimas Charitable Foundation is a Non-Governmental and Non-Profit Organization. Incorporated under the Trustees Act 1952, the foundation's mission is to provide guidance and funding in support of the welfare and the well-being of orphans and underprivileged children in Malaysia.

We have since been actively helping underprivileged children to have a chance at a normal childhood like any other children possibly could. It is about ten years ago that the foundation addressed the need of the underprivileged children via a three-pronged approach with the formation of three programmes which are Budimas Home Charity Fund (BHCF), Budimas Food Charity Fund (BFCF) and Budimas Education Charity Fund (BECF) that addresses the three basic necessities of a child which are food, education and a home.
