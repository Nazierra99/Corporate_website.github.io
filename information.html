How Can You Help?

We face many challenges and together we can overcome them. We need your help. There are the ways you can help:

Fundraising Campaign
  1. Join Budimas's monthly monetary donation(DMA)
  2. Donate books to our libraries
  3. Donate items to our homes


Be our Volunteer
  1. Be our ambassador
  2. Recommend individuals who are passionate about children to join the Budimas team.
  3. Open door for CSR campaign.
  4. Talk about Budimas to family and friends.
  5. Introduce underprivileged children to join Budimas Home.
  6. Visit/ skill training/ running activities with the children at the home/ library.
